Some important features
In the city of Hail

Al Qashla Historical Palace:

Qeshla Castle was built in 1960 AD. During the reign of King Abdulaziz, and considered one of the most important forts in the city of Hail, And distinguished by its architecture and distinctive style.

Historic city of Qaid:

This city is located 130 km from the city of Hail on the southeastern side, and is home to the Asad tribe and the Teie tribe, and the most famous monuments are the Al-Kharash Palace and the ruins of the Grand Mosque.

Hail Regional Museum:

The Museum of Hail contains a hall for the natural and geological history of the region of Hail, where it is located on an area of ​​10,000 square meters, and it displays samples of the rocks present in the region, mining sites and mines.
